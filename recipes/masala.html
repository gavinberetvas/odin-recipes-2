Chicken Tikka Masala

Recipe:

Experience the rich and vibrant flavors of chicken tikka masala, a popular Indian dish that is sure to please your taste buds. Tender pieces of chicken are marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then grilled and simmered in a creamy tomato sauce with aromatic spices. Served with fragrant basmati rice, this dish is a must-try for any lover of Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • plain yogurt
  • lemon juice
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • garam masala
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • turmeric
  • salt
  • vegetable oil
  • butter
  • onion
  • tomato paste
  • crushed tomatoes
  • heavy cream
  • sugar
  • basmati rice
  • water
  • bay leaf
  • cinnamon stick
  • cardamom pods
  • cumin seeds

directions

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place in a bowl. Add yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, garam masala, paprika, cumin, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and brush with vegetable oil. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until browned and cooked through.
  3. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, sugar, and salt. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Add the grilled chicken to the sauce and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the chicken is fully coated in the sauce.
  7. Meanwhile, rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Add the rice and water to a pot with a pinch of salt, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cumin seeds. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover. Cook for 18-20 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve alongside the chicken tikka masala. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Enjoy!
