Chicken Rice Bowl with Mango and Cucumber Salad

Recipe:

This flavorful chicken rice bowl with mango and cucumber salad is a perfect lunch or dinner option. The succulent chicken is marinated in a blend of spices, grilled to perfection and served over fluffy rice. The sweet and tangy mango and cucumber salad adds a refreshing crunch to the dish, while the creamy yogurt dressing ties it all together.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.
  2. Cook rice according to package directions and set aside.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing into strips.
  4. In a medium bowl, whisk together yogurt, 1 tablespoon olive oil, sliced cilantro, and salt to taste.
  5. To make the mango and cucumber salad, combine diced mango and cucumber in a bowl. Add chopped cilantro, salt, and pepper. Toss gently to combine.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together yogurt, olive oil, and salt to make the dressing.
  7. To assemble the bowls, divide the rice among 4 bowls. Top each with sliced chicken and a generous serving of mango and cucumber salad. Drizzle with yogurt dressing and serve.
  8. Optional: top with sesame seeds and diced roasted peanuts. Enjoy!
