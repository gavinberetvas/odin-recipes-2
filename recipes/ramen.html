Satisfying Ramen Bowl

Recipe:

Experience the comfort of a hot bowl of ramen, featuring soft noodles and savory broth. With tender slices of pork, boiled eggs, and crunchy vegetables, this dish is a perfect balance of flavors and textures that will warm your soul and leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • ramen noodles
  • pork belly or pork shoulder
  • eggs
  • baby bok choy
  • carrots
  • green onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • chicken broth
  • salt
  • pepper
  • vegetable oil

directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the pork into slices and season generously with salt and pepper. Place on a baking sheet and roast for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  3. While the pork is roasting, boil the eggs for 6-7 minutes and then run under cold water. Peel and cut in half.
  4. Thinly slice the garlic and ginger. Cut the baby bok choy and carrots into bite-size pieces. Slice the green onions.
  5. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant.
  6. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, and mirin to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  7. While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  8. Add the baby bok choy and carrots to the broth and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until tender.
  9. Divide the noodles among 4 bowls. Pour the hot broth over the noodles. Top with the roasted pork, boiled eggs, sliced green onions, and any other desired toppings. Serve hot and enjoy!
